# Product Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Business Problem

In a competitive retail environment, businesses often struggle to gain clear visibility into their sales performance, cost structures, and the impact of discounts on profitability.

Key challenges include:

* Lack of centralized view of **revenue, cost, and profit**
* Difficulty in understanding **discount effectiveness**
* Limited insights into **customer segments and buying patterns**
* Inability to track **monthly and yearly performance trends**

This project addresses these challenges by building an interactive dashboard that transforms raw data into actionable insights for better decision-making.

---

## Project Objective

To design and develop a data-driven Power BI dashboard that:

* Tracks **sales performance across products and categories**
* Analyzes **profitability and cost efficiency**
* Evaluates the **impact of discount strategies**
* Provides **time-based insights** for trend analysis

---

##  Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Data visualization & dashboarding
* **MS SQL Server** – Data extraction & transformation
* **SQL (CTE, Joins, Calculations)** – Data modeling

---

##  Project Structure

* `Products.pbix` → Interactive Power BI dashboard
* `SQLQuery1.sql` → Data transformation and business logic

---

##  Data Modeling & SQL Logic

* Implemented **Common Table Expressions (CTE)** for structured querying
* Performed **data joins** across multiple tables:

  * Product data
  * Sales data
  * Discount data
* Created calculated metrics:

  * Revenue = Sale Price × Units Sold
  * Total Cost = Cost Price × Units Sold
  * Discount Revenue after applying discount percentages
* Extracted **Month & Year** for time-based analysis

---

##  Key Insights Delivered

* 📌 Revenue and cost breakdown by product, category, and brand
* 📌 Identification of **high-performing and low-performing products**
* 📌 Impact of discounts on overall revenue
* 📌 Monthly and yearly sales trends
* 📌 Customer segmentation analysis

---

##  Business Impact

* Enables data-driven decisions to **optimize pricing strategies**
* Helps identify opportunities to **increase profitability**
* Improves understanding of **customer behavior**
* Supports stakeholders with **clear and actionable insights**
